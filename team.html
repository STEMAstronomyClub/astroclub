Meet Our Team

Get to know the passionate minds behind our astronomy club! Our team is made up of astronomy enthusiasts dedciated to exploring the cosmos and sharing the wonders of the universe. Together, we're here to inspire, educate, and make the stars a little closer for everyone.

Meet Our Founder

Abdelrahman Bayoumy

The founder of the STEM Astronomy Club and Teaching Assistant at Zewail City discovered his passion for astronomy early on. Fascinated by star alignments in the sky, he began exploring astronomy through a Facebook page during preparatory school. Using a mobile app, he learned to identify stars and constellations. In secondary school, he joined an astronomy club, where he learned more and gave lectures, inspiring him to establish the STEM Astronomy Club to empower young enthusiasts to explore this fundamental science.
